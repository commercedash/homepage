catalogIQ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Commerce Dash collects, uses, and protects Merchant information when installing and using the catalogIQ application on the Shopify platform.

2. Information Collection (Minimal Scope Setup)

catalogIQ is designed with the principle of "least privilege" collection. The App only accesses the absolute minimum necessary data needed to function. During OAuth installation, we request the following scopes:

  • read_products, write_products: To read and modify product data, tags, and variants.
  • read_inventory, write_inventory: To check stock states for complex visibility filtering.

We do not access customer personal data (PII) or order financial history.

3. Data Handling & Architecture

All catalog sync and metafield intelligence operations happen via specialized edge computing. Our approach utilizes:

  • AWS DynamoDB Caching: Ephemeral caching for rapid app load times. Cache is strictly localized to your store.
  • KMS Encryption: All sensitive backend tokens and API keys are encrypted at-rest using AWS Key Management Service (KMS).
  • HMAC Verification: All incoming Shopify requests and webhooks are cryptographically verified using SHA-256 HMAC ensuring robust spoofing prevention.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

If you choose to uninstall the catalogIQ application from your Shopify Admin:

  • Within 48 hours of receiving the app/uninstalled webhook, all permanent access tokens and shop-specific DynamoDB caches are aggressively wiped.
  • Anonymized aggregate usage data (e.g., total syncs performed) may be kept solely for internal performance and infrastructure monitoring (CloudWatch log retention rules apply).

5. Third-Party Sharing

Your store data is never sold, traded, or shared with third parties under any circumstances. Internal app processes solely interact with the official Shopify API.

If you have any specific inquiries regarding our GDPR compliance, data flows, or security models, please contact us at via our Contact Form, detailing "Compliance" in the Subject.
